Columns

- Lat, lon, h_meas: Location, Height of weather station
- d15, f15, theta15: predicted Wind direction, wind speed, potential temperature
- N2: Brunt-Vaisala frequency according to CARRA
- e<digits>: Elevation in <digits> kilometers upwind
- n_e: Number of elevation points to sea, a heuristic for distance from sea
- f: Real measurement of wind speed
